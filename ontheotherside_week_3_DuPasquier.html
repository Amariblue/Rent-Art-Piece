On the Other Side


"Art is what makes life more interesting than art"
(Robert Filliou)



Since the pandemic has begun, our access to physical relationship to art and artworks has decreased considerably, but increased our digital social and professional lives considerably. We acknowledge the necessity of being in touch through the digital realm, and we use all its precious ressources.

But exhibitions and artworks are often to be sensed first through our and their physicality: materiality, smell, size, the scale of a space or the texture matter. They inform and invite for direct contacts. A direct relationship also allows a viewer to activate not only the view as the most dominant perception sense but also all the other senses.
